Where to find us

We are 15km from Marondera, along Mahusekwa Road, 1.4km off 10 miles junction (corner of Marondera Road and Wedza Road OR corner Seke Road and Mahusekwa Road).

Directions:

Our broiler brooder house can be reached in several ways.



From Marondera CBD: Approximately 20km - 20 minutes drive

From OK Supermarket in Marondera, drive in Mutare Road and turn LEFT into Wedzwa Road.

Drive straight through up to 10 Miles junction (Corner Mutare Road & Wedza/Marondera Road) towards 10 Miles junction as if you are heading to Waddlive Primary & Secondary Mission Schools.

Upon reaching 10 Miles junction (Corner Seke Road and Wedza Road), continue going straight into Mahusekwa Road, a dust road as if you are heading to Border Church.

From 10 Miles junction, drive 1.4km in Mahusekwa Road and you reach our broiler brooder house, located to your left.

From Dema Police Station in Chitungwiza: 30km - 30 minutes drive

Drive Wedza-bound straight through in Seke Road for about 30km and you will reach 10Miles junction.

From 10 Miles junction, turn right into Mahusekwa Road as if you are heading to Border Church.

Upon reaching 10 Miles junction (Corner Seke Road and Wedza Road), continue going straight into Mahusekwa Road, a dust road as if you are heading to Border Church.

From 10 Miles junction, drive 1.4km in Mahusekwa Road and you reach our broiler brooder house, located to your left.

We look forward to welcoming you at Rooster Kings Broiler Brooder House soon!
