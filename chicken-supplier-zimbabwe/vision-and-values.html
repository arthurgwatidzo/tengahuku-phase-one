Corporate Identity

Vision

To be the customer's preferred choice of premier quality chicken, helping our valued customer to live longer, healthier lives.

Mission Statement

To produce prime chicken while adhering to the highest levels of specifications, international standards and norms in order to get both our customers satisfaction
as well as our employees.

Values

Excellence We are diligent in our work and consistently deliver the best quality in everything we do.

Innovative We are agile and flexible in our work. We continually improve the quality of the poultry we produce. We do this by continually imporving the quality of the poultry we produce.
We will do this by continually improving the breeding, feeding, housing and watering of the broilers and the envrionment in our care.

Integrity We act with honesty and integrity in the conduct of our broiler farming.

Stewardship We have a commitment to the stewardship of our broilers, our environment and our people.

Teamwork We are committed to a teamwork environment where every person is a valued member, treated with respect, encouraged to contribute and recognized and rewarded for their efforts.
