About Us

Rooster King, trading as Tengahuku, is a commercial broiler farming producer and wholesaler providing chicken at competitve prices without compromising the quality of chicken meat.
Rooster King was founded and launched in November 2018.

Construction and setting up of brooding facilities began in August 2014 and was completed in November 2018. Our premises are located in Marondera, Zimbabwe.
Our business is built on customer service and quality poultry.
We strive to produce high quality, nutritious chicken. The company uses internationally recognised and proven broiler faming techniques in the brooding of broilers. We feed our broiler using non-GMO stockfeeds.
We raise our birds in a cleaner and healthier & inspected brooding facility, regaularly engaging veterinary experts to watch over our poultry.
